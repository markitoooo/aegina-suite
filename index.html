About Us

Aegina Suite Ike is a premium property management company located in the stunning Saronic Gulf. We specialize in managing high-end hospitality properties and suites, delivering an unmatched experience for guests and property owners alike. Our approach blends local expertise with modern design and management techniques, ensuring every property reaches its full potential.
